Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Reality Check is a fun relationship-clarity app. It is not therapy, medical advice, legal advice, or emergency support.

What you enter

You may enter case details such as a name or nickname, context, answers, red-flag selections, notes, and an optional replay moment. These cases are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to share or export something.

Device permissions

  • Microphone and speech recognition are used only when you choose to dictate a replay moment.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to save or share a verdict card.
  • Notifications are used only if you allow them, for Daily drops, streak reminders, open-case updates, and re-entry nudges.

Analytics and purchases

Case content is intended to stay on your device. The app may still process app usage events, an app-specific user identifier, content attribution such as campaign or deep-link source, and purchase or subscription status through service providers such as PostHog, Apple, and RevenueCat. We use this to understand feature usage, keep purchases working, attribute installs, and improve the product. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Service providers

We may use service providers for analytics, app functionality, purchase validation, subscription status, and support. These providers process data on our behalf for the purposes described in this policy.

Sharing

When you export or share a verdict card, you control where it goes. Avoid adding real names or sensitive details if you do not want them visible to others.

Data deletion

You can delete your cases in the app settings. Deleting local data removes it from this device.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@familyjourney.app.
